Visitor Terms

Guest Invitation & Visitor Policies

Clear rules governing client presence, desk allocation limitations, and presentation suite access rules.

1. Maximum Guest Limits: Standard hot desk plans are allowed one visitor on the desk floor for up to two hours daily. Larger client cohorts must be hosted in booked meeting spaces.

2. Reception Registries: All guests must scan their digital invite barcodes at the front gate terminal. Hosts will receive SMS notifications immediately upon check-in.

3. Lounge Amenities: Registered visitors gain free access to micro-roasted coffee and Wi-Fi networks during their allocated guest hour windows.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear guidance regarding memberships, access credentials, and guest allowances.

Can I bring guests with a Hot Desk membership?

Yes. Monthly Hot Desk members receive 2 guest day passes per month. Additional guests can enter using meeting room credits or buying daily visitor passes.

How do meeting room credits work?

Every credit equals one hour in our huddle rooms. Credits renew monthly and do not roll over to the next billing cycle.

What is the notice period for corporate office cancellations?

For team office suites of 6+ desks, we request a 30-day notice period. Day passes and weekly plans expire automatically with no renewal action required.
