Compliance FAQ

LLC Verification & Notary FAQS

Important details about legal lease agreements and utility verification bills.

Can you supply a commercial lease agreement for bank compliance?

Yes. Our Professional and Corporate packages can supply commercial sublease documentation upon request, which is often required by financial institutions during credit evaluations.

Do you support US Postal Service Form 1583?

Absolutely. To legally open and scan your mail, the USPS requires Form 1583 to be signed and notarized. We provide free virtual notary service vouchers to expedite this step during onboarding.
