Availability FAQ

Desk Holds & Release Policies

Answers to specific questions regarding live seat locks and release durations.

For how long is a selected desk held in checkout?

Once you select a desk on the interactive map, it is locked in checkout for 10 minutes to allow booking details submission. If booking isn't confirmed, the seat automatically returns to available status.

Are walk-in bookings updated instantly on the map?

Yes. Turnstile entries at our branches sync immediately with our servers, updating the visual seat status on this page in real-time.
