Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, direct answers regarding memberships, access, invoicing, and space logistics.

What is included in the Hot Desk plan?

Our Hot Desk plan grants full access to open-plan working bays, high-speed fiber internet, locker rentals, unlimited espresso specialty coffee, and full access to standard member networking events.

Are there any locked-in long-term commitments?

No! We offer flexible monthly billing agreements. You can cancel your desk plan or upgrade to larger cabins anytime with a standard 30-day notice.

Can I bring guests for client presentations?

Yes. Guests are welcome if you book a dedicated meeting room. They will be registered at the reception entryway and ushered directly to your presentation workspace.

How do meeting room booking credits work?

Every desk membership receives monthly room credits. These credits reset on the first of each month. Additional meeting room reservations can be added directly via your online member dashboard.
