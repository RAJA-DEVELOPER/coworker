Venue FAQ

Event Check-In & Directions FAQ

Directions, parking validation, and check-in procedures for visiting guests.

Is parking validation provided for event guests?

Yes. We validate parking for up to 3 hours in the Innovation Street underground garage. Bring your ticket to the front reception desk during check-in.

Do non-members need to register in advance?

Yes. Security requires all visiting non-members to register for tickets in advance. You will receive an entry QR code on your registered email address.
