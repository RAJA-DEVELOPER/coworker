FAQ

Hourly Room Booking FAQ

Answers to specific questions regarding cancellations, payments, and invoices.

What happens if my meeting runs over the scheduled time?

If the subsequent slot is vacant, you can extend your booking hourly via the member app or by coordinating with the floor host. If another team has booked the space, we will assist you in relocating to a huddle room.

Are HDMI adapters and dongles provided in the rooms?

Yes. USB-C, Lightning, and HDMI adapters are permanently connected to the central table media wells. Multi-device dongles are also available at the reception desk.

Can external non-members book meeting rooms?

Absolutely. External teams can book any available meeting space hourly at standard rates. Non-members just need to register guests at the main desk upon arrival.
