Guidelines

Shared Space Guidelines

To ensure a comfortable, high-productivity environment for all builders, we enforce minor floor guidelines.

Focus Acoustics

Please route voice calls to phone booths. Quiet hours are active on standard desk rows from 9am to 6pm daily.

Guest Registration

Visitors must check in at the reception deck. Hot-desk users are allowed 1 guest for up to two hours daily.

Kitchen Courtesy

Workspace kitchen cups, mugs, and plates are fully self-service. Return cutlery to active dishwashers after meals.

Service Pets Only

Authorized, clean service animals are welcome. General pet registry options vary by district branch regulations.

FAQ Panel

Reservation & Invoicing FAQ

Answers to specific questions regarding cancellations, payments, and invoices.

Can I cancel my day reservation for a refund?

Yes. Cancellations made at least 24 hours prior to your scheduled check-in time will receive a full refund. Cancellations under 24 hours are eligible for booking credit adjustments.

Do you generate custom corporate tax invoices?

Yes. Custom corporate tax receipts (containing company VAT/tax IDs) are generated automatically and emailed immediately upon check-out.

How do I extend my booking duration mid-day?

If the slot remains unreserved, you can extend your duration hourly via the local member portal or by coordinating directly with the floor hosts at the reception entryway.
